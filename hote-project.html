Real Estate Website

Project Summary

The project involved creating a website for a client who wanted to rent out his flats. The client was already using Airbnb and Booking to advertise his flats but wanted to have his own website to avoid commission fees. The website needed to function like Airbnb, where clients could book directly and pay for their stay. Additionally, the website needed to connect with the client's calendar.

Challenge

One of the main challenges of the project was integrating the client's flat listings with a booking widget that could connect to the client's calendar on both Airbnb and Booking. This required a deep understanding of how both platforms work and the ability to integrate the data in a way that was seamless and easy to use for the client and their potential renters.

Another challenge was that the client had no existing online presence, so the project needed to be started from scratch. This required thorough planning and development, as well as creating a design that aligned with the client's vision and brand identity. The team had to spend time understanding the client's requirements and preferences to create a website that met their needs.Overall, the project required a combination of technical expertise, creativity, and communication skills to successfully deliver a website that met the client's expectations and provided a seamless experience for potential renters.

Design System and Style

We created a design system that would not only build the consistency but also save time for the client as they build new features. This will improve the overall process and experience for the website.
